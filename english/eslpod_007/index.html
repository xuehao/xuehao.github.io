No.7 Food and cooking

发表于 2019-06-30

Our topic today is Food and Cooking. Do you like cook? why or why not?

We ask this question as usual to a couple of native speakers. First we're going to hear from Mary and then I will give my response. And as usual, we'll end with some discussion of vocabulary. Let's listen it.

I love to eat, but I don't like to cook, not at all. I probably don't like cook because I'm really bad at it. When I was growing up, my mother did all of the cooking in our house. But I never did learn how to cook any dishes at all. And so when I went to college and I moved into my own apartment, I had to learn how to cook a little bit.

Usually, what I made were very simple, instant thing like I made ramen noodles. I bought a lot of TV dinners. And I also bought a lot of frozen pizzas. So I didn't have a very good diet when I was in collage. But I eat a lot of things that were easy to make, that were in my very small student budget.

Later on I did try to learn how to cook a little bit, I try to cook for a boyfriend or two. And each time I try to cook, I will get some simple recipes that required things like marinating the meat, chopping up the vegetables, getting spices to add to the dish and I will do all of those things. And I would spend a lot of time, maybe hours and hours, working on cooking a meal. And then in the end, it would always turn out badly. Whether I would cook it too long and it would burn or overcook it, or it would just simply taste terrible.

I don't think I'm made out to be a cook.
